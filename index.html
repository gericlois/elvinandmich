A Note From The Couple

Our Story

Our story began in 2013 — classmates in CPRT, sharing the same lecture halls long before we became anything more to each other. For years we were friends, seatmates, and study partners; the kind of pair people always seemed to find together. In 2019, we finally stopped pretending it was anything else and chose each other for good. Today, we still share an office — workmates by day, partners through everything else. From classmates, to lovers, to workmates, and now on May 29, to husband and wife. Hello po mga ka-PAMILYA — we cannot wait to see your faces in the room.

Elvin & Michelle
